Suites of Euston
A boutique hotel
Suites of Euston is housed in a building that dates back to 1878. The property is one of Charlottetown's most iconic structures, reflecting the architectural style and cultural heritage of the late 19th century. Originally built as a private residence, the building has been meticulously restored to preserve its historical character while providing modern comforts to guests.
